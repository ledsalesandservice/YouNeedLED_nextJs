# Social Media Strategy — AI Voice Agent Content

**Audience:** South Jersey home-service & small-business owners (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, field service, trades) and local operators who live on inbound calls.
**Objective:** Drive "AIVOICE" comments/DMs → demo conversations → booked calls.
**Primary platforms:** Instagram + Facebook (Reels cross-posted), with LinkedIn for the carousel and quote.

## Posting Cadence (suggested 2-week run)
Post 3–4x/week. Lead with the highest-impact hook (missed calls), then educate, then lower the barrier.

| Week | Day | Piece | Why this slot |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Tue | **Reel 1 — Missed Call** | Strongest hook + clear dollar stakes = best top-of-funnel opener |
| 1 | Thu | **Carousel — AI vs. Hiring** | Educates + handles the "why not just hire someone" objection |
| 1 | Sat | **Reel 3 — Tier-1 Requests** | Weekend = owners reflecting on the week's chaos; pain-point resonates |
| 2 | Tue | **Reel 2 — Call to Ticket** | Product proof; shows the "how it works" after interest is warm |
| 2 | Thu | **Quote — Start Small** | Low-friction CTA to convert warm-but-hesitant viewers |
| 2 | Sat | **Reboost Reel 1** (Story/repost) | Recycle the top performer; retarget non-converters |

**Best time-of-day (Eastern):** 7–9 AM (before the workday) and 5–7 PM (after jobs wrap). Owners in the trades check phones early and evening, not mid-day.

---

## Posting Notes by Piece
- **Reels (1, 2, 3):** Burned-in captions are mandatory — assume muted autoplay. Front-load the hook in the first 3 seconds. Keep one clear CTA: comment "AIVOICE."
- **Carousel (2):** Strong cover slide drives the swipe. Put the comparison table (slide 5) as the "payoff." End on the CTA slide.
- **Quote (5):** Designed to be saved/shared. Pair with a longer caption and the same comment CTA.
- **Every post:** Reply to every "AIVOICE" comment fast (ideally within the hour) and move to DMs. Pin a comment with the CTA on each Reel.

---

## CTA Consistency
Single, repeatable action across all content: **Comment "AIVOICE"** → auto/manual DM the walkthrough → book a demo. One keyword keeps tracking clean and trains the audience.
