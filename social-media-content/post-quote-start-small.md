## Body / Caption
Most owners put off automation because they think it's an all-or-nothing switch. It isn't.

Pick **one** thing the AI Voice Agent handles first:
- After-hours calls
- Appointment confirmations
- Overflow when your line is busy
- Tier-1 questions (hours, pricing, service area)

Get comfortable. See the results. Then expand to the next use case.

Small start, compounding wins. That's how you modernize without disrupting what already works.
