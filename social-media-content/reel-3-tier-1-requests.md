## Full Script

**(0:00–0:03) — Hook**
"Your $80-an-hour technician just spent 20 minutes on a question a robot could answer."

**(0:03–0:12) — The problem**
"'What are your hours?' 'Do you service my area?' 'How much for a tune-up?' 'Is my appointment still on?' These are Tier-1 calls — simple, repetitive, and constant. Every one pulls a skilled tech or dispatcher off real work."

**(0:12–0:22) — The cost**
"Do that a dozen times a day and you've lost hours of billable time — and interrupted the flow on every job. The expensive people are answering the cheapest questions."

**(0:22–0:27) — The fix**
"Let the AI Voice Agent handle every Tier-1 call: hours, service areas, pricing, scheduling, confirmations. Your techs only get pulled in when it actually needs a human."

**(0:27–0:30) — CTA**
