## The Math (for the on-screen breakdown)
- Average job value: **$5,000**
- Missed calls per week: **5**
- Close rate on answered calls: **1 in 3**
- Weekly lost revenue: 5 × (1/3) × $5,000 = **~$8,300**
- Annual lost revenue: **~$130,000+**
