The Reality Check

Your Current Camera System May Be Giving You False Confidence

Most homeowners and business owners in South Jersey have one of three situations:

01
No Cameras At All

You know you need them, but you've been putting it off. Every week that passes is another week your property is unprotected.

02
Consumer Cameras (Ring, Arlo, Nest)

They look good in the app, but they record in short clips, depend entirely on cloud subscriptions, compress footage so heavily it's often unusable as evidence, and go offline when you need them most.

03
Old, Outdated System

Analog cameras from 10+ years ago with blurry footage, dead hard drives, and no remote access. You think you're covered. You're not.

The reality: When something happens — a break-in, a slip-and-fall, a theft, a vandalism incident — the quality of your footage determines whether you get justice or get nothing. Evidence-grade recording is not a luxury. It's the difference between a solved case and a closed file.
